THE LENS

A final-year student of Film & Digital Production, with four years of academic work and seven years of professional creative experience — no formal design education required.

The work spans documentaries, short films, video art, installations, research, PR writing, and public speaking. All of it connects back to one drive: to use media as a vehicle for impact.

The goal isn't just to tell stories. It's to build pathways — for local talent, for global reach, for a more literate and connected Bangladesh.

Project 11 — Documentary

SPRAY OF
COURAGE

Documentary Film Summer 2025 Cinema Vérité

A Cinema Vérité documentary following Sadman — a graffiti activist from Bangladesh's July Revolution who stayed true to the cause when others turned away. Shot with archive footage, projections, and a poetic Bengali voiceover written for the film. The most challenging and most meaningful production in the portfolio.
